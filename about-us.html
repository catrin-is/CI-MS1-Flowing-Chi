About Flowing Chi

Flowing Chi is run by Florence Ryder (Flo). Flo began her Tai Chi journey at the age of 30 when she joined a local class to help her relax and switch off from the anxiety and stress of life. She found the flowing gentle movements cathartic and from there developed an interest in the origins of Tai Chi and Qigong as well as researching the health benefits. Flo’s background is in health and education, she is passionate about helping people to improve their health and wellbeing by reducing anxiety and stress and promoting good posture and balance, both physical and mental. She is also keen to advocate the gentle, meditative movements of Tai Chi and Qigong as an accessible health and well-being maintenance programme for all ages and abilities.

Flo is a qualified Session Leader and continues to develop her own learning, experience, and knowledge. She runs both face to face and online sessions which are relaxed and friendly. Sessions are mainly held at the Community Forum in Penparcau and Flo’s sessions are delivered outside whenever the weather permits. Keep an eye out on Social Media for her outdoor pop-up events.
