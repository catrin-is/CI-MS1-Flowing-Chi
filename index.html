Tai Chi & Qigong

Tai Chi and Qigong are ancient Chinese practices that have led to improved health, fitness and wellbeing. They both combine slow movement, meditation and breathing to promote the movement of Qi (energy). Tai Chi is a series of connected forms in a sequence, which are learnt. Qigong is made up of gentle, repeated movements, which are led and easily followed.

Health Benefits

  • Boost your wellbeing and mood

  • Relax your body with gentle movements

  • Improve balance and increase flexibility

  • Develop your focus and concentration
